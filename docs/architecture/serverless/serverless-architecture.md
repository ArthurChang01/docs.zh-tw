<span data-ttu-id="a6a05-182">IoT 是指一起聯網的物理物件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-182">IoT refers to physical objects that are networked together.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-183">它們有時稱為"連接設備"或"智慧設備"。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-183">They're sometimes referred to as "connected devices" or "smart devices."</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-184">汽車和自動售貨機的所有產品都可以連接併發送從庫存到感應器資料（如溫度和濕度）的資訊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-184">Everything from cars and vending machines may be connected and send information ranging from inventory to sensor data such as temperature and humidity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-185">在企業中，IoT 通過監視和自動化提供業務流程改進。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-185">In the enterprise, IoT provides business process improvements through monitoring and automation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-186">IoT 資料可用於調節大型倉庫中的氣候或通過供應鏈跟蹤庫存。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-186">IoT data may be used to regulate the climate in a large warehouse or track inventory through the supply chain.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-187">物聯網可以感知化學品洩漏，並在檢測到煙霧時致電消防部門。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-187">IoT can sense chemical spills and call the fire department when smoke is detected.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a6a05-188">設備和資訊的絕對量往往要求一個事件驅動的體系結構來路由和處理消息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-188">The sheer volume of devices and information often dictates an event-driven architecture to route and process messages.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-189">無伺服器是理想的解決方案，原因如下：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-189">Serverless is an ideal solution for several reasons:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-190">隨著設備和資料量的增加，實現擴展。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-190">Enables scale as the volume of devices and data increases.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-191">適應添加新端點以支援新設備和感應器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-191">Accommodates adding new endpoints to support new devices and sensors.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-192">便於獨立版本控制，以便開發人員無需部署整個系統即可更新特定設備的業務邏輯。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-192">Facilitates independent versioning so developers can update the business logic for a specific device without having to deploy the entire system.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-193">恢復能力和減少停機時間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-193">Resiliency and less downtime.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a6a05-194">IoT 的普及導致多個專門關注 IoT 問題的無伺服器產品，例如[Azure IoT 中心](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/iot-hub)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-194">The pervasiveness of IoT has resulted in several serverless products that focus specifically on IoT concerns, such as [Azure IoT Hub](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/iot-hub).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-195">無伺服器自動執行設備註冊、策略實施、跟蹤甚至將代碼部署到*邊緣*設備等任務。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-195">Serverless automates tasks such as device registration, policy enforcement, tracking, and even deployment of code to devices at *the edge*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a6a05-196">邊緣是指連接到 Internet 但不屬於 Internet 活動的一部分的感應器和執行器等設備。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a05-196">The edge refers to devices like sensors and actuators that are connected to, but not an active part of, the Internet.</span></span>
