 [用戶端互通性](../../../../docs/framework/wcf/samples/client-interoperability.md)

 [位址標頭](../../../../docs/framework/wcf/samples/address-headers.md)

 [具類型用戶端](../../../../docs/framework/wcf/samples/typed-client.md)
